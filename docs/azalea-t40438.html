After basics

In words, either we have (9)r5c5 xor
no 5 at r19c5,r5c19, and this implies (5)r1c9=(5)r9c1 (Fireworks)
so no 5 at r9c9, which in turn implies (5)r9c1,(5)r1c9 resulting in no 5 at block b1.
Therefore, +9 r5c5; ste
